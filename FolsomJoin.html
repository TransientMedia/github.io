HIRING INFORMATION

The Bureau of Land Management's Veteran Crews are, "specifically designed and configured to employ veterans and provide career opportunities in fire and other land management or natural resource careers." It is for this reason that it is our goal to hire veterans in our crewmember positions. Typically we receive over 100 applications every year.

Ocassionally we do have openings in the GS-6/7 grades for Squad Leaders that we hire non-veterans for. These positions are for the most qualified individuals with a fire resume that ensures they are prepared to adequately mentor and train our veteran crewmembers.

OUR IDEAL CANDIDATE

We are looking for individuals with grit, a strong work ethic, and the ability to excel in a crew environment. A year-round dedication to physical fitness is also necessary as we travel and work in the most rugged areas of the country.

FITNESS

Success in our program requires a dedication to physical fitness. The BLM Minimums are provided, but crewmembers routinely far-exceeds these standards.

To ensure a healthy season it is important to start training early.

2019 Crew Averages
1.5mi Run: 9:16
Pull-ups: 22
Sit-ups: 105
Push-ups: 99
3mi 45lbs "Pack Test": All Passed.

BLM Minimums
1.5mi Run: 11 minutes or less.
Pull-ups: 7 in three minutes.
Sit-ups: 45 in three minuntes.
Push-ups: 25 in three minutes.
3mi 45lbs "Pack Test": Less than 45 minutes.
